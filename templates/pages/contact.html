✦ FAQ

Common Questions

Can't find what you need? Visit our Help Centre →

Is InvoiceFlow completely free? +

Yes — InvoiceFlow is 100% free to use. Create invoices, download PDFs, and access your full history with no hidden charges or subscriptions, ever.

Can I use InvoiceFlow on my phone? +

Absolutely. InvoiceFlow is fully responsive and works smoothly on mobile, tablet, and desktop. Your invoices and history sync to your account across all devices.

How do I add my company logo to invoices? +

Log in, go to Profile Settings, and upload your logo. It will automatically appear on all future invoices. PNG or SVG format is recommended for the best quality output.

Is my invoice data secure? +

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. We never share or sell your information. You can export or permanently delete your data at any time from account settings.

Can I edit an invoice after saving it? +

Yes. Any invoice in your history can be reopened, edited, and saved as a new version. You can also duplicate invoices for recurring clients to save time.
