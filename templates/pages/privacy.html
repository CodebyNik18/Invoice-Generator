Your Privacy
Matters to Us

InvoiceFlow is built for people who want their invoice data to stay theirs. This page explains what we collect, why we collect it, and the controls you have over your account.

Last updated: April 19, 2026 Effective: April 19, 2026 About 8 min read
Plain-English Summary

We collect only what we need to run your account and generate invoices. We do not sell your data, and you can request deletion of your account data at any time.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use InvoiceFlow, we collect the minimum information needed to provide the service safely and reliably.

Account information includes your name, email address, and password, which is stored as a secure hash rather than plain text.

Profile and invoice data includes your business details, uploaded logo, billing information, client information, dates, line items, and totals.

Usage and technical data includes device, browser, IP address, and basic activity signals so we can improve performance and protect accounts.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to operate, improve, and secure InvoiceFlow.

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Generate, save, and retrieve invoices you create
  • Send service emails such as verification and password reset messages
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access
  • Understand broad usage patterns so we can improve the product
  • Meet legal or compliance obligations when required

We do not use your personal data for advertising, resale, or unrelated profiling.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. We share it only in limited cases where it is necessary to run the service or follow the law.

  • Service providers: trusted vendors such as hosting or email providers that help us operate InvoiceFlow
  • Legal requests: when disclosure is required by law, regulation, or court order
  • Business transfers: if InvoiceFlow is merged, acquired, or reorganized, with equivalent privacy protections applied
  • Your consent: if you explicitly authorize us to share data for a specific purpose

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect data in transit and at rest. That includes HTTPS, access controls, secure password handling, and tightly limited internal access.

No system can promise absolute security, but we work to reduce risk and respond quickly if something goes wrong.

If you believe your account or data has been compromised, email security@invoiceflow.in as soon as possible.

5. Cookies

We use a small number of cookies and similar technologies that are necessary for core features such as authentication and maintaining your session.

We do not use advertising cookies or invasive third-party tracking on this page. Disabling essential cookies may affect how the app works.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data.

  • Access: request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: update inaccurate account information
  • Deletion: ask us to permanently delete your account and data
  • Portability: receive your invoice data in a usable format
  • Objection: challenge certain uses of your data when applicable

To make a request, contact privacy@invoiceflow.in. We aim to respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

We keep your account data and invoices while your account is active so you can continue using the product without losing your records.

After you delete your account, we remove personal data and invoices within 30 days unless a longer retention period is legally required.

Anonymized, aggregated product analytics that cannot identify you may be retained longer to help us improve the service.
